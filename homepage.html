ShopSmart 4 Health aims to show you how to shop for and prepare healthy and affordable meals all year round. In this first edition we outline a healthy diet, which includes:

    plenty of fruit and vegetables (including legumes such as chickpeas, lentils and beans), breads, pasta, rice, noodles and cereals (preferably wholegrain) moderate amounts of reduced fat milk, yoghurt and cheese, lean meats and poultry, fi sh, eggs and nuts small amounts of foods such as chocolate, chips, soft drinks and fast foods on occasion, if at all drinking plenty of water.

Eating a diet high in fruit and vegetables is essential for good health. Here at Deakin University, we’ve been researching what Victorian women eat and found that over half don’t eat sufficient fruit and vegetables for good health. We know that eating healthy foods is not always easy when you are on a tight budget or trying to please the rest of the family. We will give you lots of tips to make healthy eating easier with affordable ideas that taste great too!
