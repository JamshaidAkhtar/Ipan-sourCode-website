  • Here are our best tips to make you save time:


    • 1- Add fruit and vegetables to every meal and snack, when eating out, entertaining friends and at work. Think about variety and the different colours of the rainbow. Always have cut up fruit and vegetables within easy reach!

    • 2- Check your pantry, freezer and fridge. Stock these with basic items (Newsletter 2), which form the base of almost any meal. This makes cooking easier and quicker.

    • 3- Be a super shopper. Write a shopping list and take it with you! Buy fruit and vegetables in season or tinned and frozen when they are not.

    • 5- Keep food safe. Think safety when preparing and storing your food to avoid contamination and waste.
